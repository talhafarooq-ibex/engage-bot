ABOUT THE ENGAGE RAG

This project leverages cutting-edge language processing technology. It specifically designed to provide accurate and insightful answers to users' questions from a number of documents. We utilized advanced language modeling techniques to create embeddings, which are representations of words and phrases that capture their context and meaning. These embeddings enable our model to understand the nuances of language and generate responses that are contextually relevant and accurate to the documents.

Powered by a Language Learning Model (LLM), it is constantly learning and improving its knowledge base about the targeted company. It analyzes vast amounts of data, including company reports, news articles, customer reviews, and social media interactions, to gain a comprehensive understanding of the company's operations, products, and services. This enables it to provide users with detailed and up-to-date information, helping them make informed decisions or gain insights into the company's offerings.
